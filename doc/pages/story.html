Introduction

Blob Wars : Blob And Conquer is a mission and objective based 3D action game. The player assumes the role of a Blob solider called Bob and must fight there way through various environments to complete missions and progress the story line.

Blob Wars : Blob And Conquer is Episode II in the Blob Wars Saga.

Story

Blob Wars : Blob And Conquer takes place a short time after the end of the previous installment, Metal Blob Solid.

With the apparent defeat of the enemy leader, Galdov, the Blobs have regained their confidence in winning the war and have uncovered a secret hidden on their own homeworld! Four Crystals known as the Fire, Time, Space and Reality Crystals were being hunted by the alien invaders. However thanks to the bravery of a Blob called Bob the Crystals were kept out of enemy hands.

Bob now leads the Blobs in a mission to take back their homeworld from the aliens invaders who are still present and put an end to the war once and for all. With his knowledge of combat, Bob intends to lead the Blobs across their homeworld in a series of strategic strikes on enemy strong holds, supply lines and bases.
