Trouble Shooting

I've just installed a new version of the game and my controls, etc. seem to have become all messed up!

This is probably due to configuration changes. Try deleting your config file,

$HOME/.parallelrealities/blobAndConquer/config

You will have to reenter your preferred controls, etc. but this should solve the issue.
