Completing Objectives

Completing objectives is the key to finishing missions in Blob and Conquer. Objectives can range from collecting a number of items to defeating a particular enemy. Once you have completed an objective you will recieve an on screen notification to let you know that you have done so.

To view objectives open the Status window. The objectives that are valid for the mission you are on will be listed there. There are two types of objective, Primary and Secondary.

Primary Objectives are the mission's main objective. In most cases they must be completed before you can proceed on to the next level.

Secondary Objectives can be completed if the player wishes. Whilst not needed to finish the level they will usually provide the player with some kind of reward for the effort.
